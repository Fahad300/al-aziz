Our Equipment & Services

Our facility features precision Side Seal and Bottom Cutting machines for clean edges and heavy-duty strength. We pair this with high-speed Flexo Printing to deliver vibrant, professional branding on every package.

Extrusion

High-quality extrusion where raw materials are melted and blown into consistent, durable plastic films—perfect thickness, clarity, and strength for its intended use.

Printing

Using advanced high-speed machinery to apply vibrant, custom designs with color accuracy and sharp detail—turning standard packaging into a powerful branding tool.

Cutting

Precision cutting where films are shaped into specific bag styles—side-seal for retail or bottom-seal for heavy-duty industrial use—with clean edges and reinforced welds.

Who We Are

Established in 2010

Established in 2010, our company has grown into a premier leader in Pakistan's packaging industry. By maintaining a wholly-owned production facility, we ensure uncompromising quality control and the ability to manufacture superior-grade plastic solutions tailored to our clients' needs.

We specialize in high-performance materials, including HDPE, LDPE, and PP film grades, and have earned a distinguished reputation in the flexographic printing market. Our reach extends to major industrial sectors nationwide.
