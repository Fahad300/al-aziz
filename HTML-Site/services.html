Our Services

Process Flow

Extrusion → Printing → Cutting

01

Extrusion

Our process begins with high-quality extrusion, where raw materials are melted and blown into consistent, durable plastic films. This stage ensures the base material has the perfect thickness, clarity, and strength for its intended use.

02

Printing

Using advanced high-speed machinery, we apply vibrant, custom designs to the film. Our printing process focuses on color accuracy and sharp detail, turning standard packaging into a powerful branding tool for your business.

03

Cutting

The final stage involves precision cutting, where films are shaped into specific bag styles. Whether it is side-seal for retail or bottom-seal for heavy-duty industrial use, we ensure every edge is clean and every weld is reinforced.
