Materials Used

Film Grades

LDPE / PP / BOPP

LDPE

LDPE is a flexible, high-clarity material renowned for its superior tear resistance and reliable moisture protection.
  • › Offers a soft, stretchable texture that offers superior puncture resistance and durability.
  • › Provides great transparency, ensuring your products are clearly visible and professionally presented.
  • › Acts as a reliable shield against dust and humidity to keep contents safe and clean.

PP

PP is a high-clarity, high-strength material known for its stiff texture and brilliant glossy finish.
  • › Offers exceptional "glass-like" clarity, making it the perfect choice for showcasing the true colors of textiles and retail goods.
  • › Possesses a higher melting point than other plastics, allowing it to maintain its shape and integrity in warmer environments.
  • › Provides a crisp, firm feel that gives packaging a premium look and prevents it from wrinkling or stretching easily.

BOPP

BOPP is a high-grade plastic film stretched in two directions for maximum strength and glass-like clarity.
  • › Offers a crystal-clear, high-gloss finish that makes product colors pop and allows for perfect visibility on retail shelves.
  • › The biaxial stretching process creates a stiff, durable film that resists stretching, tearing, and punctures even under heavy handling.
  • › Provides a robust shield against water vapor and humidity, ensuring that food, textiles, and dry goods stay fresh and protected.
