Material Used

High-Performance
Film Grades

We source and process only top-grade raw materials from trusted suppliers to guarantee superior output across every product line.

LDPE

Flexible LDPE Film
  • Flexible, high-clarity film with excellent tear resistance.
  • Moisture protection to help keep contents clean and well-preserved.
  • Reliable performance for everyday industrial and textile packaging.

PP

High-Strength PP Film
  • High-clarity, high-strength glossy finish for premium presentation.
  • Heat resistant performance for consistent packaging stability.
  • Ideal for packaging applications requiring a firm, premium feel.

BOPP

BOPP Biaxial Film
  • Biaxially oriented for maximum strength and long-lasting durability.
  • Glass-like clarity that helps colors look vivid and true.
  • Water vapor barrier protection for dependable shelf and transit performance.
